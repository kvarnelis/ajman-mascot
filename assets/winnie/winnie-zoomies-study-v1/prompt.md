This must look kinetically different from an ordinary directional run. Build a chaotic zoomies cycle from sharply contrasting compressed and stretched silhouettes, sudden direction changes, planted or skidding paws, a highly active curved/upright tail used for balance, playful arched spine, excited alert face, and quick changes between forward, airborne, sideways, and pivoting body orientation.
